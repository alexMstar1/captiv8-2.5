Mission

To be your most valuable partner

Captiv8 was built to empower brands & creators to harness the data-science behind authentic storytelling. We built our proprietary platform based on our real-world campaign experience.

What started as a tool to help us scale our impact evolved into our secret sauce as we broke the mold of “Managed Service” or “SaaS Platform.” We believe you can have both.

Culture

We love what we do

We see work as a place where our passion and careers collide. We are fitness junkies, busy moms, plant whisperers, home cooks & are connected by a love of storytelling. Sound like you?
